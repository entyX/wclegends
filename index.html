In versus, P1 uses WASD + E pass, Space kick, Q lob, left Shift sprint. Mouse aiming is solo-only — in versus both players aim in the direction they're running.

Who you control

You are the ball. Whoever on your team holds it, you play as them. Complete a pass and you instantly become the receiver. If an opponent intercepts, you stay as the player who kicked it — chase back and win it! Your player wears a gold ring and name tag.

The rules

Offside — a teammate clearly ahead of the second-last defender (and the ball) in the opposing half when you pass can't be first to it. Whistle → free kick against you.
Fouls — mistimed slides & barges concede a free kick, and reckless slides can earn a yellow card.
Stamina — every player has their own fitness and tires through the match, so late legs slow down — manage your sprints.
Squads — full 11-a-side. Set a custom shape via Edit formation in My Squad, or drag players on the pause map.
Out of play — sidelines give a kick-in; goal-lines give a corner or goal kick.
Group games — 90 minutes; can end in a win, loss or draw.
Knockouts & qualifiers — win or bust: level after 90' → extra time to 120', then a penalty shootout.
Switching players — press 1–0 / - to lock onto a shirt number; press it again for dynamic (follow-the-ball) control. In singleplayer you always attack the right-hand goal.
XP — goals, assists, wins and clean sheets earn XP. Level-ups grant skill points to spend in My Squad.
Contracts — every squad member signs for 1–3 World Cups. Expired players leave at season's end.
